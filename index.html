About

I love to create! Whether it's building a great website, putting all the pieces of a song together or cooking a new recipe, creating things is my jam.

I started learning how to code in 2019 and taught myself a lot of the basics through Codeacademy and freeCodeCamp. After that, I went through the program at DevStar Academy to hone my front-end skills. Next, I went to LaunchCode and worked through their full-time 14 week CodeCamp and got into full-stack development. I continue to code, build and learn more every day!

When I'm not staring at my computer screen I like to make music, play with my dog, Ace, and destroy my friends in nerdy board games.
